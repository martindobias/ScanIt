URL switches

encode
Download as encoded file, only "base64" value is allowed
source
Select different TWAIN source; obtain source list through Image sources page
x
The x-coordinate of the upper-left corner of the crop rectangle; positive, higher than or equal to 0
y
The y-coordinate of the upper-left corner of the crop rectangle; positive, higher than or equal to 0
w
The width of the crop rectangle; positive, higher than 0
h
The height of the crop rectangle; positive, higher than 0

Please note, actual crop is defined as intersection of desired crop rectangle and the image. Should the intersection be empty, whole image is provided.
